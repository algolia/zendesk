Algolia for Zendesk

Empower your Zendesk Help Center with Algolia Search and provide your users a Google-like search experience.

Join the beta

This JavaScript library allows you to replace the default search of your Zendesk Help Center by Algolia. Algolia is a hosted full-text, numerical, and faceted search engine capable of delivering realtime results from the first keystroke.

We'll crawl your Zendesk API to extract your Help Center content and provide you a small code snippet to power your search with Algolia.

Features

Auto-suggestions menu

Offer End-Users immediate access to your whole help center from the dropdown menu, whatever your number of sections or articles.

Instant & Faceted Search

Have your search results page, navigation and pagination updated in realtime, after each keystroke.

Relevance

Increase customer satisfaction by serving relevant results.

Customizable

Our code is open-source and hosted on GitHub, feel free to customize it.
